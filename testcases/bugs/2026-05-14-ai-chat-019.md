# BUG-019: Credit balance not decremented after successful sends

**Date:** 2026-05-14
**Suite:** 05-ai-chat
**Severity:** Critical
**Test Cases:** TC-AI-CHAT-CREDITS-001, TC-AI-CHAT-CREDITS-008

## Description
After multiple successful AI chat sends (26 confirmed in usage log), the `dailyRemaining` balance in `GET /billing/balance` remains at 49 (only 1 deducted from initial 50). Credits are being logged in the usage table (`credits_consumed=1` per row) but the running balance is not being decremented to match.

## Evidence
- 26 entries in `GET /billing/usage` each with `credits_consumed: 1`
- Balance before testing: `dailyRemaining: 49, monthlyRemaining: 500`  
- Balance after 26 sends: `dailyRemaining: 49, monthlyRemaining: 500` (unchanged)
- Usage log has 26 rows for today's session

## Expected
After 26 sends starting at dailyRemaining=50: dailyRemaining=24, monthlyRemaining=474.

## Impact
Critical billing issue. Users can consume unlimited AI resources without their quota being enforced. The credit enforcement middleware deducts from balance (allowing first send to work), but the balance reflection in the balance API is stale or the deduction is being rolled back/compensated incorrectly.
