# BUG-2026-05-14-publish-004: Billing page shows negative monthly credits usage (-400 / 100 used)

**Severity:** Medium  
**Environment:** dev (https://dev.doable.me)  
**Date:** 2026-05-14  
**Affected TCs:** N/A (billing UI)

## Summary
The billing page displays "Monthly Credits: -400 / 100 used" with a negative used value of -400. The credit usage bar also appears fully filled (purple bar), which is inconsistent with negative usage.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Log in as owner-pro@doable.me
2. Navigate to /billing

## Expected
Monthly Credits shows "X / 100 used" where X is a non-negative integer (0–100).

## Actual
Monthly Credits: -400 / 100 used  
Progress bar: 100% filled (purple)  
Available credits: 549

## Impact
Confusing UX — users see a negative number for credits used. The 549 available credits also seems inconsistent with a 100 monthly plan limit.

## Notes
The "549 available" figure in the top right may be a total across daily + monthly + rollover credits (49 + 500 + 0 = 549), which could explain the apparent discrepancy. The negative monthly usage (-400) suggests a data calculation bug where rollover or bonus credits are being subtracted from usage incorrectly.
