# BUG-2026-05-14-publish-007: /dashboard URL intermittently redirects to /billing or /usage

**Severity:** Medium  
**Environment:** dev (https://dev.doable.me)  
**Date:** 2026-05-14  
**Affected TCs:** TC-PUBLISH-LIFECYCLE-025

## Summary
Navigating to `https://dev.doable.me/dashboard` intermittently redirects to `/billing` or `/usage` instead of staying on the dashboard. This makes it unreliable to navigate to the dashboard directly.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Log in as owner-pro@doable.me
2. Navigate to https://dev.doable.me/dashboard
3. Observe URL after page load

## Expected
URL stays at /dashboard, dashboard page renders with project list.

## Actual
URL changes to /billing or /usage on some navigations. The page content matches the redirected URL.

## Frequency
Observed 3 times during test session. Reproducible intermittently.

## Notes
May be related to the Next.js router and how the authentication state resolves. The sidebar navigation clicks may be registering before the router stabilizes, causing unexpected navigation. Alternatively, a middleware redirect rule may be incorrectly sending authenticated users from /dashboard to /billing.
